Research Interests

My research focuses on developing machine learning models and algorithms for statistical analysis of user behaviors over social networks and social media sites. It has practical applications in motivating users for online engagement, user profiling for targetted advertising and more efficient recommendations. My recent projects include the followings:
  • Modeling user behavior over social question answering sites
  • Modeling the diffusion of competing products over social networsk
  • Modeling the content over social media
  • Link prediction in aligned heterogeneous networks
